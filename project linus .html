Linus Torvalds

The Creator of Linux

Biography

Linus Torvalds is a Finish-American who is the creator and principle developer of the Linux Kernel for a number of operating systems. He also created the version control system Git.Torvalds was born on
December 28,1969 in Helsinki,Finland.He studied computer science at the university of Helsinki, and he developed the Linux as a student project in 1991. Since then, the Linux Kernel has become one of
the most widely used kernels in the world, powering everything from smartphones to supercomputers.
